New Zealand Study Guide for Students - All you need to know!

New Zealand earned a reputation as a friendly and most happening country in the world, which is what attracts the global students to come and study at affordable prices. And New Zealand is a combination of both metropolitan and countryside landscapes with beautiful sceneries.

Why Study in New Zealand?

Here are the benefits of studying in New Zealand for Indian Students.

  • The most prominent educational system in the world
  • A perfect balance of theoretical and practical modules centered on industry-specific training Qualifications that are widely recognized and respected on a global scale
  • Education is less expensive in comparison in the United States and Europe.
  • Fees are only due upon visa approval.
  • More Student visas in comparison to countries such as the United States
  • Hospitality programs that include paid internships
  • Engineers can benefit from low-cost industry-endorsed programs.
  • Because of the scarcity of skilled workers in various industries, there is a high demand for new graduates.
  • A temperate climate with pleasant seasonal changes makes it ideal for study and recreation.
  • In the last ten years, a large number of Indians have successfully migrated.

Here is the approximate cost to study in New Zealand for the Indian students.

  • Undergraduate degree - $20,500 - $25,000 annually
  • Master's degree - $19,000 - $29,000 annually
  • Doctoral degree -$6,500 - $9,000 annually
  • MBA -$31,000 - $50, 000 annually

To study bachelor's or masters in New Zealand, a set of eligibility criteria has been set by the New Zealand Universities.

  • For Undergraduate program – Valid 10+2 standard education or equivalent
  • For Masters –Valid Bachelors degree or equivalent
  • Qualifying IELTS/TOEFL Score
  • English Language proficiency
  • Good Academic score
