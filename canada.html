Study in Canada for Students - All you need to know!

Canada education is renowned for being a global leader in research and academic rigor while at the same time continuing to be accessible to people from different backgrounds. The higher education programs in Canada are more flexible and student-friendly than most programs across the world. Many students appreciate this flexibility and opt for Canada for their masters, making it one of the most popular destinations for international education all over the world.

One can choose from more than 200 universities and colleges to complete a bachelor's or higher degree in Canada. Students can complete an undergraduate degree in four years, and take a further two years to complete their post-graduate degree in Canada.

Benefits of Studying in Canada:

Canada is being the top-notch choice for most international students for many decades. Here are the reasons, why.

  • Excellent quality of education and high academic standards made Canada one of the most favored study abroad destinations.
  • A high standard of living attracts most international students to study and settle in Canada.
  • Affordable tuition fees and cost of living are other major factors to choose Canada for higher studies.
  • Generous Scholarships for students who are eligible and are in need.
  • A plethora of job opportunities for skilled workers and graduates are major advantages of students who finished graduation in Canada.
  • Experiment-based learning along with classroom training to focus more on practical knowledge is important for students which can be achieved in Canada.
  • Students can experience a diverse student community as Canada is a hub for students from all parts of the world.
  • Students can apply for permanent residence after their education in Canada.

Students can work up to 20 hours per week during the classes and 40 hours during holidays and vacations. This is one of the greatest advantages of studying in Canada.

IELTS General and CELPIP are the two English language proficiency test scores accepted in Canadian Universities. Even to enter Canada, an IELTS score is mandatory to prove your proficiency. Citizenship and Immigration Canada recognized IELTS as a qualifying test to assess the ability.

Canada is the hub of opportunities for graduates. Here are some of the top-paying jobs in Canada.

  • Mechanical Engineering for 50,000 CAD or above
  • Data Science & Machine Learning for 65,000 CAD or above
